Hodson Bay Hotel & Spa

Hotel with superb rooms, fine dining and a lively waterfront bar. There’s a 20-metre swimming pool, a children’s pool, steam room and sauna. For an extra special treat, the Spa at Hodson Bay is second to none. Enjoy a treatment then top it off with a soak in the outdoor hot tub while you bask in the views over the lake.

Modern and elegant with free parking and 68 spacious bedrooms, this 4-star hotel is among the best in Athlone. Family-friendly, there are excellent facilities for families including kid’s camps and a children’s pool at the leisure centre.

With a bird’s eye view from its Iconic tower of where the Shannon meets Lough Ree, at the 4-star Sheraton Athlone you’ve got it all. Right in the centre of town, it’s just a stone’s throw from the best shopping, restaurants, bars and all the town’s main attractions.
