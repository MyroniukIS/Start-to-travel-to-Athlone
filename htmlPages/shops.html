Glasson Craft Gallery

Glasson Craft Gallery

Situated in the heart of the Village of the Roses, Glasson Craft Gallery offers a unique and varied range of local Irish Craft. Ranging from handmade jewelry and accessories, local pottery and ceramics, and artisan food. The Art Gallery provides an exhibition space for Irish artists. There is also a bespoke picture framing service available.

Burgess Department Store

Burgess Department Store

Burgess Department Store, founded in 1839, is famous for being the longest established department store in Ireland. This Irish family run retail emporium is renowned for premium customer service, quality fashion & homeware brands, delivering customers a unique & modern shopping experience.
